SCRAMBLED EGGS

a not-so-simple staple you have to get right.

Managing heat, sourcing quality eggs, and timing this dish right is key.

Recipe

Ingredients
  • eggs
  • butter
  • cracked black pepper
  • a slice of sourdough to toast

Steps
  1. Toast sourdough to taste
  2. Heat Pan to medium heat
  3. Heat butter until just bubbling
  4. Crack egg into pain and break yolk
  5. Allow to cook for 15 seconds then fold
  6. Fold again, cook for another twenty seconds
  7. If just slightly wet, transfer to room temp plate.
